Plan Your Perfect Trip

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Malaysia is between March and October, when the weather is dry and ideal for sightseeing and island adventures.

Transportation Tips

Public transport is reliable in big cities. Use Grab for ride-hailing, and take domestic flights or buses to travel between states affordably.

Cultural Etiquette

Respect local customs, dress modestly in rural or religious areas, and always remove your shoes before entering someone’s home or a mosque.

Must-Try Foods

Don't miss Nasi Lemak, Char Kway Teow, Roti Canai, and Laksa. Try hawker stalls for authentic and affordable local flavors.
